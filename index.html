How to Make This App Private
Option 1 — Built-in Login (Free, already active)
Your app already has a login screen. Only people with your credentials can access it. Change them in Settings.

Option 2 — Netlify Password Protection

1
Go to app.netlify.com and open your site
2
Click Site configuration → Access control
3
Under Visitor access, enable Password protection
4
Set your password and click Save. Netlify will require it before anyone can load your app.
Recommendation: The built-in login is sufficient for personal use. Change the default password in Settings right away.
